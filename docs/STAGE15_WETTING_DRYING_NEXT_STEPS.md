# Stage 15 wetting and drying next steps

The positivity limiter prevents negative depth but does not by itself complete a production wetting-and-drying model．The following independent Verification tasks remain before any connection to real Onga River data．

1．Cell activation and deactivation hysteresis near the dry threshold．
2．Momentum reset and velocity regularisation in newly wet and nearly dry cells．
3．Well-balanced wetting over variable bathymetry．
4．Boundary inflow into initially dry cells．
5．Gate and fishway structure fluxes coupled to wet and dry states．
6．Mesh-scale convergence on synthetic shoreline advance and retreat problems．

No real bathymetry，roughness，tide，river discharge，fishway discharge，or gate opening is selected at this stage．This file records the next numerical Verification sequence and does not authorise physical calibration or geometry changes．
